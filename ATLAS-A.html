VR Safe Space

Since creating spaces for VR aren't particularly user-friendly for people who aren't already experience with GameDev, I wanted to create/compile assets that could be used to easily make a VR "Safe Space" for people with trauma/PTSD, anxiety, etc who might find themselves in a situation in which they feel uncomfortable and need an escape. While not everyone has a portable VR system (Oculus Quest in my case), this could be scaled down to be used with smartphones and more affordable phone VR goggles.
