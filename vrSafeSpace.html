VR Safe Space

Since creating VR spaces is not really user-friendly for people who aren't familiar with GameDev, my initial goal was to create a tool to enable people to easily place prefabs and assets to create their own virtual reality environment. However, I needed to scale down my scope and instead decided to begin creating and compiling free assets to enable people to create these environments instead. Specifically, I wanted to develop "safe spaces" for people with trauma/PTSD, anxiety, etc. who might find themselves in a space where they are uncomfortable and need a familiar escape. While not everyone has or can afford a portable VR machine (such as the Oculus Quest), this could also be scaled down with low-poly prefabs and used on a smartphone with affordable VR goggles (such as Google Cardboard).
