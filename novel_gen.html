LLM Writing Exercise Generator

I wanted to develop a fun way to do creative writing excercises, and came up with this generator. It will take either input or random genres, themes, and tones, and then return a novel title and synopsis. I set up a semantic embedding space for the genre, theme, and tone lists using bert-base-uncased, and then used cosine similarity to compare the words in my random selection code. Then I tuned the hyperparameters of my search helper function to output words are neither synonyms, antonyms, or otherwise incompatible. There's lots of things to use it for - I use it to practice building worlds, characters, scenes, etc. You can try out the tool in HuggingFace Spaces here, or you can view colab notebook with expanded capabilities here
