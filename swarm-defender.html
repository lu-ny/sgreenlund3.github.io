Swarm Defender

An arcade style spaceship dogfighting game made entirely in javascript using p5.js and p5.play. I wanted to create enemies that exhibit rudimentary flocking behavior and chase the player down. The bottom ground color is randomly selected every time the page loads, and it saves your high score. I'd like to add in actual sprites, music, and a variety of powerups and enemy types.
